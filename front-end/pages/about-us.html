FAQs

FoodMate is a service that connects local food businesses to people. FoodMate has always brought people to things that matter to them, and we are now committed to bringing things that matter closer to people. When you place an order with FoodMate, we receive it, send it off to the restaurant and assign a FoodMate delivery partner to pick up the order to bring it to you.
Depending on your area and local restaurant hours, we begin delivering as early as 7am and run as late as 10pm.
To see which restaurants are available for delivery, just enter your address in FoodMate. To keep your food fresh and ensure speedy deliveries, we limit the available restaurants to those within a given distance from your location.
Cost of the food depends on what the restaurant is charging for their food plus a delivery fee you can see in the FoodMate website!
FoodMate presents a variety of food based on what is available around you, Vietnamese, Western, Asian, European, Fusion and so on. We will continue to bring on more variety of food as we expand.
